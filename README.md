ScamGuard AI – Intelligent Fraud Detection & Response Assistant
Overview

ScamGuard AI is a modern AI-powered web application designed to help users identify, understand, and respond safely to online scams and phishing attempts.

The platform provides intelligent scam analysis, safe email response generation, scam research capabilities, recovery planning, and an interactive AI assistant. Its goal is to improve digital safety by helping users recognize fraudulent communications and make informed decisions before sharing sensitive information or taking risky actions.

Problem Statement

Online scams are becoming increasingly sophisticated through:

Phishing emails
SMS scams
WhatsApp scams
Fake job offers
Investment fraud
Cryptocurrency scams
Social engineering attacks

Many users struggle to distinguish legitimate messages from fraudulent ones.

ScamGuard AI addresses this challenge by providing AI-powered analysis, educational insights, and recommended actions to help users stay protected online.
